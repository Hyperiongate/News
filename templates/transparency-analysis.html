Transparency Recommendations

Add Conflict of Interest Statement
Include a clear disclosure about any relationships between the author and subjects covered in the article.
Explain Data Methodology
When citing statistics, explain how the data was collected and analyzed to help readers evaluate its reliability.
Implement Corrections Policy
Display a clear corrections policy and maintain a log of any updates or corrections made to the article.
Provide Supporting Documents
When possible, link to or embed primary source documents that support key claims in the article.
