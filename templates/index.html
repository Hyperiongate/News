How to Use This App

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Find a transcript from a speech, interview, podcast, or video (see sources below)
  2. Copy the transcript text to your clipboard
  3. Paste the text into the text area below
  4. Click "Analyze Transcript" to start the AI analysis
  5. Review the results - you'll get credibility scores, bias detection, fact-checking, and more

What Gets Analyzed

  • Factual Claims: AI extracts and verifies specific claims made in the transcript
  • Content Credibility: Evaluates the overall trustworthiness of statements
  • Bias Detection: Identifies potential bias in language and framing
  • Transparency: Assesses clarity and honesty of communication

Tips for Best Results

  • Use complete transcripts (not partial snippets)
  • Include speaker names/timestamps if available
  • Longer transcripts (500+ words) provide more comprehensive analysis
  • Clean text works best - remove excessive formatting

How to Get YouTube Transcripts

  1. Open any YouTube video in your browser
  2. Click the "..." (more) button below the video
  3. Select "Show transcript" from the menu
  4. Copy all the text from the transcript panel
  5. Paste it into the text area above

Not all videos have transcripts. If you don't see the option, the creator hasn't enabled it.
