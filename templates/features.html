Comprehensive Analysis Features

Advanced AI-powered tools to analyze news, transcripts, and live streams with professional-grade accuracy

Features (12 Tools)

Source Credibility

Goal: Determine if a news outlet is trustworthy and reliable.

How it works: Analyzes outlet's founding history, awards (Pulitzer), bias ratings (AllSides, MBFC), fact-checking record, and ownership transparency.

Output: Trust score (0-100), bias rating, credibility assessment, and specific concerns

Bias Detection

Goal: Identify political lean and loaded language in article.

How it works: AI scans for partisan keywords, emotional language, one-sided framing, and sensationalism. Compares against neutral reporting standards.

Output: Bias meter (left/center/right), examples of biased language, overall fairness score

Fact-Checking

Goal: Verify if factual claims in the article are true.

How it works: Extracts claims, cross-references with trusted databases (FRED, BLS, WHO, CDC), and verifies using 4-AI consensus (GPT-4, Claude, DeepSeek, Cohere).

Output: Each claim marked as verified/disputed/unverifiable with sources and evidence

Author Analysis

Goal: Assess journalist's credibility and expertise.

How it works: Researches author's publication history, awards, credentials, expertise areas, and potential conflicts of interest using AI and databases.

Output: Author credibility score, biography, expertise assessment, publication history

Transparency

Goal: Measure how openly the outlet discloses methods and sources.

How it works: Checks for source attribution, methodology disclosure, corrections policy, funding transparency, and conflicts of interest.

Output: Transparency score, specific areas of concern, comparison to journalism standards

Content Quality

Goal: Evaluate depth of research and professional standards.

How it works: Analyzes writing quality, source diversity, depth of evidence, logical structure, and adherence to journalistic standards.

Output: Quality score, strengths/weaknesses analysis, comparison to professional benchmarks

Manipulation Detection

Goal: Identify emotional manipulation and propaganda techniques.

How it works: Scans for misleading headlines, cherry-picked data, emotional appeals, logical fallacies, and propaganda patterns (fear-mongering, scapegoating).

Output: Manipulation risk score, specific techniques identified, examples with explanations

Transcript Analysis

Goal: Fact-check speeches, interviews, and video content.

How it works: Extracts transcript (YouTube URL or paste text), identifies claims, verifies facts using FRED economic data + AI consensus, analyzes speaker quality.

Output: Claim-by-claim fact-check with sources, speaker credibility score, PDF export

AI Council

Goal: Get multiple AI perspectives on any question.

How it works: Simultaneously queries 10 different AI services (GPT-4, Claude, Mistral, DeepSeek, Cohere, Groq, xAI, Perplexity, Reka, AI21) with your question. Generates consensus summary.

Output: 10 AI responses, agreement/disagreement areas, consensus score, auto-extracted claims

Media Literacy Quiz

Goal: Test and improve your ability to spot misinformation.

How it works: Interactive quizzes on clickbait detection, bias identification, fact vs opinion, and source evaluation. Three difficulty levels with instant feedback.

Output: Score, correct answers with explanations, personalized learning path, achievements

Claim Tracker

Goal: Build searchable database of verified claims.

How it works: Automatically extracts 5 verifiable claims from every analyzed article using Claude AI. Stores in database with verification status and sources. Fully searchable.

Output: Searchable claim database, verification status, source links, appearance tracking

Debate Arena

Goal: Engage in structured debates on news topics.

How it works: Create debate topic + your argument (300 words max). Others join opposing side. Community votes on who made the stronger case. Live results displayed.

Output: Debate status, argument quality, vote results with bar charts, moderator controls

Authoritative Data Sources

AllSides Media Bias Ratings

Comprehensive bias ratings for 1,400+ news sources, continuously updated by editorial review and community feedback. Tracks left/center/right lean across the political spectrum.

Media Bias/Fact Check (MBFC)

Detailed fact-checking and bias analysis of 5,000+ sources. Provides credibility ratings, ownership transparency, and historical accuracy tracking.

Pulitzer Prize Database

Complete records of journalism's highest honor since 1917. Tracks winning publications, categories, and exceptional reporting standards.

Pew Research Center

Nonpartisan research on media trust, consumption patterns, and public opinion. Gold standard for understanding news credibility and audience perception.

NewsGuard Trust Ratings

Professional journalist reviews of news sources based on nine criteria of credibility and transparency. Covers thousands of major news websites.

Comprehensive Data Sources

Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED)

845,000+ economic time series from 108 sources. Real-time data on GDP, unemployment, inflation, interest rates, and market indicators for fact-checking economic claims.

Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS)

Authoritative source for employment data, wages, productivity, and workplace statistics. Monthly reports on job creation, unemployment rates, and labor market trends.

World Bank Open Data

Free access to global development data including poverty rates, GDP, education, health metrics, and infrastructure indicators across 200+ countries.

CDC & WHO Health Data

Comprehensive health statistics, disease surveillance, vaccination rates, mortality data, and public health trends from authoritative medical organizations.

Scientific Journals & Research Databases

Access to peer-reviewed research from PubMed, arXiv, JSTOR, and major scientific publications. Verifies scientific claims against published research.
