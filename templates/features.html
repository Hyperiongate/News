Comprehensive Analysis Features

Advanced AI-powered tools to analyze news, transcripts, and live streams with professional-grade accuracy

Features

Source Credibility

Evaluates news outlet reliability based on founding history, awards, bias ratings, and track record. Database of 500+ sources with detailed metadata.

Bias Detection

Identifies political lean, sensationalism, loaded language, and editorial slant. Provides visual bias meter and specific examples.

Fact-Checking

Verifies factual claims against trusted databases and sources. Highlights questionable statements and provides evidence-based corrections.

Author Analysis

Assesses journalist credentials, publication history, awards, and expertise. Provides context on author's background and potential conflicts of interest.

Transparency

Evaluates source attribution, methodology disclosure, conflicts of interest, and corrections policy. Measures editorial transparency standards.

Content Quality

Analyzes writing quality, depth of research, source diversity, and professional standards. Identifies thin or poorly researched content.

Manipulation Detection

Identifies emotional manipulation, misleading headlines, cherry-picked data, and propaganda techniques. Flags manipulative content patterns.

Transcript Analysis

Analyzes speeches, interviews, and videos for credibility. Extracts claims, verifies facts, assesses speaker reliability. Supports YouTube URLs and text transcripts.

Media Literacy Quiz

Test your skills at spotting clickbait, bias, and misinformation. Three difficulty levels with progressive challenges to sharpen your critical thinking.

Claim Tracker

Automatically extracts and tracks verifiable claims from analyzed articles. Search claims, view verification status, and link to fact-check evidence.

Debate Arena

Start debates on news topics and let the community decide. Create arguments, challenge opposing views, and vote on who made the stronger case.

Authoritative Data Sources

AllSides Media Bias Ratings

Comprehensive bias ratings for 1,400+ news sources, continuously updated by editorial review and community feedback. Tracks left/center/right lean across the political spectrum.

Media Bias/Fact Check (MBFC)

Detailed fact-checking and bias analysis of 5,000+ sources. Provides credibility ratings, ownership transparency, and historical accuracy tracking.

Pulitzer Prize Database

Complete records of journalism's highest honor since 1917. Tracks winning publications, categories, and exceptional reporting standards.

Pew Research Center

Nonpartisan research on media trust, consumption patterns, and public opinion. Gold standard for understanding news credibility and audience perception.

NewsGuard Trust Ratings

Professional journalist reviews of news sources based on nine criteria of credibility and transparency. Covers thousands of major news websites.

Comprehensive Data Sources

Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED)

845,000+ economic time series from 108 sources. Real-time data on GDP, unemployment, inflation, interest rates, and market indicators for fact-checking economic claims.

Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS)

Authoritative source for employment data, wages, productivity, and workplace statistics. Monthly reports on job creation, unemployment rates, and labor market trends.

World Bank Open Data

Free access to global development data including poverty rates, GDP, education, health metrics, and infrastructure indicators across 200+ countries.

CDC & WHO Health Data

Comprehensive health statistics, disease surveillance, vaccination rates, mortality data, and public health trends from authoritative medical organizations.

Scientific Journals & Research Databases

Access to peer-reviewed research from PubMed, arXiv, JSTOR, and major scientific publications. Verifies scientific claims against published research.
