About TruthLens

AI-powered truth analysis for news and transcripts

Our Mission

In an era of information overload, TruthLens helps you cut through the noise. We combine multiple AI services to provide comprehensive, objective analysis of news articles and transcripts—helping you make informed decisions about what you read, watch, and share.

Our mission is simple: bring clarity to complex information. Whether you're analyzing a breaking news story, verifying a political speech, or fact-checking a YouTube video, TruthLens gives you the tools to evaluate credibility and detect bias.

How It Works

TruthLens doesn't rely on a single AI model—we use an ensemble approach that combines 7 specialized analysis services:

  • Source Credibility: Evaluates outlet reputation, track record, and bias history
  • Bias Detection: Identifies political lean, loaded language, and framing tactics
  • Fact-Checking: Cross-references claims against verified databases and trusted sources
  • Author Analysis: Examines writer credentials, expertise, and past work
  • Transparency: Checks for proper sourcing, citations, and disclosure of conflicts
  • Content Quality: Assesses writing quality, logical coherence, and completeness
  • Manipulation Detection: Flags emotional manipulation, clickbait, and sensationalism

Each analysis is scored independently, then combined into an overall Trust Score that gives you a quick assessment of credibility.
