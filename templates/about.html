About TruthLens

AI-powered truth analysis for news and transcripts

Our Mission

In an era of information overload, TruthLens helps you cut through the noise. We provide comprehensive, AI-powered analysis of news articles and transcripts to help you understand credibility, detect bias, verify facts, and make informed decisions about the content you consume.

Our mission is to promote media literacy and critical thinking by making professional-grade news analysis accessible to everyone.

How It Works

TruthLens combines multiple AI models with comprehensive databases to analyze content across seven key dimensions:

  • Source Credibility: Evaluates outlet reputation and history
  • Bias Detection: Identifies political lean and sensationalism
  • Fact-Checking: Verifies claims against trusted sources
  • Author Analysis: Assesses journalist credentials and expertise
  • Transparency: Measures source citations and accountability
  • Content Quality: Evaluates depth and completeness
  • Transcript Verification: Specialized fact-checking for spoken content

All analysis is powered by advanced natural language processing, machine learning, and a database of over 500 news outlets with detailed credibility metadata.
